All-in-One Kitchen

The OvenPlus combined with pizza oven and top-down grill. Comprises with an upper pizza oven and a lower grill. The upper ceramic tray can reach up to 840°, so you can even set a cast-iron skillet on top and use it as a regular stove. You can cook anything from pizza, steaks, burgers, fish and lobster to a sunny-side-up egg, baked potatoes and seafood risotto—all with the OvenPlus!

A Kitchen On the Go

The OvenPlus is much smaller than a regular American grill, it’s your best choice for tailgating, camping and cookouts!

Patent-Pending Butterfly Burner

OvenPlus is designed with Butterfly burner. Different from traditional tubular burner, the plate shape burner offers multiple benefits:

Smokeless

The Butterfly burner is placed above the grate, which prevents fat from dripping into the burner and significantly reduces the smoke that typically comes from grilling meats. Excess fat flows down and collected in the fat tray for easy cleaning.

Energy efficient

OvenPlus boasts energy efficiency of up to 80% and can heat up from 0° to 500° degrees in 10 minutes, achieving multiple high-performance applications through thermal circulation. Ventilation holes allow heated air in the oven to rise and create an even convection with the guide channel, ensuring a consistent cooking temperature.

Easy Grilling

The consistency of cooking temperature allow you to grill easily. You won’t ever have to worry about undercooked or overcooked portions of the same piece of meat, every meat comes juicy and tender with preserved inside moisture. In addition, tests have revealed that it takes the OvenPlus only 15 minutes to cook steak for 8 people!

Follow the exclusive OvenPlus recipes, and you will never have to hover over your grill, wondering what’s going on with your food. Just set a timer and you can recreate dishes from exclusive restaurants.
